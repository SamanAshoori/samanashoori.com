Launched a PERN Web App

Rate my take - tinder based interface for reviewing hot takes.

Features

  • Submit Takes and Swipe on Others:Look - everyone wants to know that their opinion is literally gold - why not have empirical data to back it up by seeing how many other people agree with your takes
  • Postgre SQL:For storing user data, takes and cookie based sessions
  • Built in Authentication and Session ID:Passwords are stored securely and you stay signed in based on cookies
  • React Frontend: Now - Disclaimer - I am not a react lover however it is popular has plenty of support
  • Express.js backend: Sticking to full javascript for my web app so I dont have to learn integrating with other languages.
