Meet My New Discord Bot: The LFG Bot!

So far - the biggest trend for me in 2025 is spamming and overusing the saying "LFG" - so I decided to make a bot that does it for me.

Features

  • Hypeman: Sends LETS GO whenever you end a message with !!!.
  • Multi Gif Ability: Alternates between multiple GIFs to keep things "fresh and exciting, easy to add gifs to the list
  • Easy to Use: Just type a message ending with !!!, and the bot does the rest!
  • Built by me: Created using Python and the discord.py library.
